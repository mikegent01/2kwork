The Enchanted Guest List for the Sir Jones Estate

Sir Eryndor the Elf Knight

Appearance: Tall, slender with pointed ears and a regal demeanor. His silver armor gleams with emerald accents, and he carries a sword forged in elven forges.

Personality: Noble and disciplined, Sir Eryndor is a fierce protector of the weak. His calm demeanor hides a fiery resolve when his friends are in danger. He believes in honor above all.

Item: Powder

Lady Thalia the Sorceress

Appearance: Mysterious and enchanting, with long black hair and violet eyes. She wears a flowing dark purple robe adorned with arcane symbols, and carries a staff of obsidian.

Personality: Wise, enigmatic, and calm, Lady Thalia is deeply connected to the mystical forces. She often speaks in riddles and has a deep sense of responsibility toward the balance of the world.

Item: Water

Gorath the Flamebringer

Appearance: A towering figure, with fiery red skin and glowing yellow eyes. His armor is adorned with flame motifs, and his hair seems to flicker like fire itself.

Personality: Hot-headed and passionate, Gorath is a warrior at heart who believes in the power of fire to both destroy and purify. Despite his fierce nature, he has a protective side for those in need.

Item: Fire

Finnian the Half-Faun Bard

Appearance: Short and agile with small horns on his head, hooves for feet, and a bright smile. His clothes are made of leaves and soft furs, and he carries a flute made of wood.

Personality: Charismatic and playful, Finnian is full of energy and optimism. His music brings joy to all, and he has a knack for turning any situation into an opportunity for laughter.

Item: Seed

Lira the Forest Nymph

Appearance: Ethereal with green-tinted skin, her long hair weaves through vines and flowers. She wears a dress made of leaves and is often barefoot, with delicate wings that shimmer like sunlight.

Personality: Gentle and nurturing, Lira has a deep connection to the natural world. She is incredibly empathetic and often listens before she speaks, preferring peace and harmony.

Item: Wood

Windcaller Naria

Appearance: Slender and graceful with flowing silver hair that moves as if in a perpetual breeze. Her robes appear to flutter as if caught in an eternal wind.

Personality: Calm and serene, Naria has the wisdom of ages. She speaks softly but carries great influence, often using her powers to calm storms—both literal and metaphorical.

Item: Fan

Thalion the Ice Wielder

Appearance: Tall and composed, with pale skin and icy blue eyes. His robes glimmer with frost, and ice crystals form around his hands when he casts spells.

Personality: Reserved and calculating, Thalion’s calm demeanor reflects his deep control over the cold. He believes in using precision and patience in all things.

Item: Ice

Selene the Winter Witch

Appearance: Beautiful, with silver hair and skin like alabaster. Her robes are adorned with snowflakes that glisten like diamonds in the moonlight.

Personality: Mysterious and introspective, Selene is deeply attuned to the cycles of nature and the seasons. She often speaks in a soft, haunting tone that evokes the quiet of winter.

Item: Snow
